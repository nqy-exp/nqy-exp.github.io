Why I use AI-assisted translation here, I would like to share some of my honest thoughts.

First, I must be honest: learning foreign languages is not my natural talent. Compared to studying science, I am much slower at learning languages. There is so much to learn in the world, and my energy is limited. I cannot be perfect in every field.

To me, language is a tool. Its purpose is to show my thoughts and experiments to the world. Moving from daily conversation to a native level takes a huge amount of effort. While my English and German are good enough for daily life (my experience living and studying in Germany proves this), using very advanced or perfect words would be difficult and would take too much of my energy.

I chose AI-assisted translation so more people can see my research, and also because I know my own limits. This is not just "dumping" text into an AI dialog box; I always read through and check the results carefully. To me, if I have an efficient tool but don't use it, or use it without saying so, that would be dishonest. In research, deep thinking is much more important than beautiful language. If I had to write everything in English, my thinking would be limited by the language, which would slow me down. I am also more comfortable thinking deeply in my native language. AI translation helps me save a lot of time, allowing me to keep my thinking quality high while sharing my work with the world.

This is why I choose AI-assisted translation. It is my choice for efficiency and my way of staying honest.

P.S. Of course, I am still improving my English and am preparing for the IELTS to prove my language skills.

关于为什么在这里使用 AI 辅助翻译，我想分享一些我的真实想法。

首先，我必须坦诚：外语学习并非我的天赋所在。相比于钻研科学知识，我在语言学习方面的效率要低得多。面对无穷无尽的知识，我的精力有限，无法在每个领域都做到精益求精。

对我而言，语言是一种工具，目的是将我的想法和实验过程展示给全世界。从掌握日常交流到达到母语水平，所需的精力是巨大的。虽然我的英语和德语足以应对日常生活（我的留德经历也能证明我的语言学习能力），但如果要求词汇高级、精准无误，对我来说确实有些困难，也将耗费我难以承受的精力。

为了让更多人看到我的想法和探索研究，同时也是出于对我的精力边界的认知，我选择了 AI 辅助翻译。但这并不是简单地把文本丢给AI对话框，我会在翻译后进行仔细的通读与核实。对我而言，如果明知有提升效率的工具却弃之不用，或者使用了却隐瞒不报，那就是一种不诚实。我始终认为，在科研工作中，思考的深度远比语言的华丽更为重要。如果强迫自己每一篇都用英语撰写，我的思维会被语言的桎梏所限制，从而削弱我的效率。同时，我也更习惯用母语进行深度的思维构建，AI辅助翻译会让我节省大量的时间，在保证思考质量的同时，实现国际化表达的最优路径。

这就是我选择 AI 辅助翻译的原因。这是我对效率的选择，也是我对诚实的坚持。

P.S. 当然，我也在持续精进自己的英语水平，目前正在准备雅思考试，以进一步验证我的语言能力。
